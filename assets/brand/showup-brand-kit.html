Logo Wordmarks

The full showup. wordmark in all official colour variations. Use SVG for web and design work. Use PNG for social media, documents, and email signatures.

Clear space rule: Always maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the letter "s" in the wordmark. Never place the logo on a background that makes it hard to read.

The Mark

The period mark — a square, faithful to Sora's geometric period. Use when the full wordmark is too wide (favicons, app icons, small spaces). The coral square IS the brand at its most reduced.

The mark is never circular — Sora's period is a square, and the mark must always match. Don't round the corners or turn it into a circle.

Social Media Assets

Ready-to-upload assets for every major platform. Each downloads at the correct full resolution. PNG recommended for social media uploads.
