Reading the three
one of these is Side B
Option 7. Most dramatic. The eggplant has real presence. Peach-coral red pops against it in a way that's almost neon. Lilac blue tones it down nicely. This is the "stylized" choice. If showup. is supposed to feel like an art object, this speaks that language.

Option 8c. Most structurally distinctive. Warm content, cold sidebar, and the stripe bridges them. Reads as intentional two-tone design. Can feel like a Wes Anderson color treatment (complimentary, not harmonious).

Option 8d. Most continuous with Side A. Least showy, most livable. Reads as "showup. in the evening" rather than a different showup. Diary lives here quietly.

My gut, still: 8d. But 7 is the one that might actually stop someone scrolling through app-of-the-day lists. Depends if you want Side B to be an experience (7) or a home (8d). 8c is the middle, clever option.
