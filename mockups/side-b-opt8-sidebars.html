8c · FAVORITE?

NOTES
Reading the five
what's the Option 8 you actually want
8a is the current, safe, quiet default.

8b is Option 8 as an architectural dark dashboard. The active ink-pill is less of a color leap.

8c is Option 8 fully committing to its identity. Two-tone maroon + teal, structural, most distinctive, most 90s-bar. My personal favorite of the five if we're being bold.

8d is the warmest version. Cozy, harmonious, least contrasty. Best for long reading.

8e is the most "standard dark-mode" option. Opaque panel, no magic. Reliable.

My gut: 8c or 8d. 8c if you want Option 8 to earn its own identity. 8d if you want showup. to feel warm above all else.
