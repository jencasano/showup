What this spec covers

Summary of changes

  • Badge palette. Coral dropped from Side A. 7 swatches. Side A leads with Mixtape red (#C3342B), Side B leads with salmon (#F07A5E). Each side excludes the other side's hero red.
  • Side A palette: red, yellow, cognac, blue, teal, green, mauve.
  • Side B palette: salmon, yellow, cognac, teal, green, mauve, dusty rose.
  • Markers reordered. Square first (Mixtape red period feel). Mixtape 4-point sparkle second. Then circle, heart, check, x, squiggle.
  • Sparkle star. Uses the exact path from the sidebar motif in index.html. Matches the floating bg motifs and the motif above the wordmark.
  • Next button. "Next →" becomes "Next, →" with a comma. No em-dash, no dash-feel.
  • Tokens. All modal colors already read from tokens. Palette swap is a code-level change in js/month-setup.js, per-theme.
Deferred to STICKER_REPAINT_SPEC.md: the stickers (sunflower, muscle, flame, etc.) stay as-is in this spec. They will be repainted in the Mixtape palette in a separate, focused pass. This keeps the monthly setup polish shippable without gating on artwork.
