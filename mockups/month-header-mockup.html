Design decisions
Option A locked. Editorial inline. Big Fraunces, left-aligned, not sticky on desktop. Left-aligned with nav on right for mobile.

No stats in the header. The Mixtape reference had on-pace %, habits, and streak. Dropped -- they duplicate My Progress below.

Streak removed from month bar. The status banner already communicates momentum.

Status banner stays. Contextual (current month only), dynamic (changes per completion state), doesn't exist elsewhere.

"day X of Y" replaces stats. Small mono counter gives temporal context. Hidden on past months.

Nav arrows + calendar picker always present. All navigation controls preserved.
