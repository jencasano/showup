OPTION 6

NOTES
Reading the four
one of these should win, or confirm none of them do
Option 6 (Violet) is the "bedroom" option. Personal, dreamy, diary-friendly. Warm text on cool background is the key trick.

Option 7 (Magenta) is the "theater" option. Dramatic, stylized, loudest personality. Reddest red deviation though.

Option 8 (Maroon + teal) is the "90s bar" option. Warmest of the four, closest to Side A continuity. The teal accent is the differentiator.

Option 9 (Deep teal) is the "workshop at midnight" option. Coldest, most architectural, furthest from Side A. If Side B should be "a different mood, not a dark Side A," this is the strongest candidate.

Also keep in mind: nothing we've built is wasted. Anything we don't pick for Side B becomes a paid theme candidate later.
