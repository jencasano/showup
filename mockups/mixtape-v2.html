01
Texture levels · scroll and compare
four panels, same content, different background treatment
How to read this. Each panel has the same little card centered inside. Only the background changes. Look at the area around the card. Level 1 is static and grainy. Level 2 adds a slow, almost imperceptible warmth drifting across. Level 3 adds tiny drifting motifs (stars, dots, squiggles in faint brand colors). Level 4 is animated film grain. Pick what you want for showup.

Accessibility note. All animated levels (2, 3, 4) respect prefers-reduced-motion. Users who've set their OS to reduce motion will get the static version automatically. Nobody gets nauseated. Nobody gets battery-drained.

Body font · Inter vs Manrope
same copy, same size, different body face
Inter is quieter and more screen-optimized. Manrope is slightly warmer and more geometric, with softer letterforms. Read both passages out loud in your head. Pick the one that sits better for daily reading.

What to react to
three decisions, then we lock the foundation
Texture: which level? 1 (grain only), 2 (grain + drifting warmth), 3 (grain + warmth + motifs), or 4 (animated grain). My vote: 2.

Body font: Inter or Manrope? My vote: Manrope, because the slight warmth pairs better with Fraunces and the paper base. But honestly, both work.

Nav A cleanup: the sidebar is now organized into Your space (My log, Diary), Community (Following, All), Extras (Shop, Settings), with the Side A theme pill at the bottom. Sections feel right? Naming feel right? Should Settings be here, or in a gear icon elsewhere?
