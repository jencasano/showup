00 -- Design concept

The Quiet Room

Every empty state is a room that's been set up and is waiting. Not broken, not sad, not missing data. Just quiet. Three layers, progressively applied depending on the surface.

Three treatment tiers:

Full Quiet Room -- big standalone empty surfaces (feed, people tab, all tab). Gets all three layers: motif backdrop, warmth glow, Fraunces italic headline, and optional dashed action frame.

Nudge card -- persistent CTA that lives inside a populated view (bottom of People sidebar). Dashed border with subtle motif, Fraunces italic copy, compact CTA button.

Inline empty -- small section empties (Showing Up = 0). Just the Fraunces italic line. No backdrop, no frame.
