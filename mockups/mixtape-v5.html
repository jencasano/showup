Mixtape · v5 · stripes + A/E hybrids

showup. / adding identity to A, marrying it with E

Four panels. A with a full-page-top stripe. A with a sidebar-only stripe. A's structure mixed with E's pill treatment. E's rail with more visual confidence. Pick the one.

V1
A · with full-page-top stripe
the ribbon across the top of the book
What this is. Original A, untouched, with a thin red-yellow-blue stripe spanning the entire top of the page from left to right. It reads as the ribbon around a book, not a sidebar decoration. This is the cleanest way to add a signature without introducing a panel-divider feeling.

V3
Hybrid · A's column with E's pill treatment
frosted sidebar, nav as pills with small mono numbers, ink-pill active state
What this is. A's column structure, but the sidebar is now frosted and translucent (you can see the page texture through it), and nav items are pills with a small mono track number on the left. Active item is a full ink-black pill with paper-white text. Color stripe at the top of the page. All the elements of A kept (full column, all items visible), plus E's liveliness.

V4
Hybrid · E with more confidence
rail includes logo and stripe · active label always inline
What this is. E's floating rail, but it now anchors: the showup logo sits inside a round ink dot at the top of the rail, a tiny color stripe crowns it, and the active item always shows its label inline as a dark pill sliding out to the right, no hover required. Non-active items still show labels on hover. Floating logo with italic tagline sits at top-left of the main area. Edge rail is fixed to the left edge and always visible. This is the one that should feel most like a personal object sitting on a page.
