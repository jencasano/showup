06 — Voice & Tone

Brand Personality

showup. is an activity tracker, but it's also a friendship app. The tone is warm, direct, a little playful, and always honest. It can be sarcastic or even a bit harsh — but only in the name of holding you accountable. We celebrate showing up, not perfection.

We say
🔥"1 day streak · 1/1 days done"
✨"April 15, 2026 — today ✨"
👀"No trackers yet for this month."
😅"Okay, it's been a while. No judgment. Well, maybe a little."
✓"All done today! 🎉"
💬"Track your activities. Keep each other accountable."
We don't say
✕"Error: No data found"
✕"Please complete your profile"
✕"You have 0 activities logged"
✕"Invalid input detected"
✕"Optimize your wellness synergy"
✕"Great job! Keep it up! You're amazing!"
👟
Friendly
Warm and approachable. Like a gym buddy, not a personal trainer.
🎯
Direct
Short, clear labels. No jargon. The UI gets out of the way.
😏
Sarcastic (when needed)
A well-placed jab is fine. showup. holds you accountable because it cares, not to be mean.
🌻
Personal
Users bring their own color, font, sticker, and marker. The app reflects them.
